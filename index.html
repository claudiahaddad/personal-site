About

I'm drawn to early, ambiguous spaces, and to the challenge of shaping emerging primitives into products people actually understand and use. Across my work, I've launched crypto earning products, built growth systems that scaled to millions, and led the migration of millions of users to a new trading platform.

More recently, I've been focused on building new interaction layers onchain—developing chat, mini apps, and AI agents that make crypto more social, dynamic, and accessible to everyday users.

I studied Cognitive Science at the University of Pennsylvania, with a focus on computation, cognition, and consumer psychology. I care deeply about making complex technology feel simple, intuitive, and approachable.
